FAQ

Good to know

What makes MyRingBack different?

It's built for home service providers and done entirely for you. We set it up on your existing number, handle the texting compliance, turn every missed call into a booked job, and there's no contract. One thing, done right.

Do you handle the texting compliance?

Yes. US business texting requires carrier registration (A2P 10DLC) and consent rules (TCPA). We handle the setup and registration for you so you stay compliant, most DIY tools leave that headache on your plate.

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. No annual lock-in and no big upfront setup fee.

Is this a robot answering my phone?

No robot voice. When you miss a call, MyRingBack sends a fast, friendly text that asks what the caller needs and books the job.

Do I need to change my phone or buy equipment?

No. Nothing to install. We set everything up on your existing number, going live takes about 2 days.

What if it doesn't work for me?

That's what the 7-day free trial is for. If it doesn't turn missed calls into jobs, you don't pay, cancel anytime.
